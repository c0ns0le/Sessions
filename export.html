Sessions Export

This page provides a shell script to export your saved sessions to a textual JSON format.

  1. Click here to download the sessions-exporter.zip archive.
  2. Double-click the expanded sessions-exporter.command script.
  3. Check that the output is as expected.

If all goes accordingly, your saved sessions should be exported to a sessions-XXXXXXXXXXXXXX.json text file on your Desktop. Further steps (archival, conversion, importation, etc.) are up to you.
